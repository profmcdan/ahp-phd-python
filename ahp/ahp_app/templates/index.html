Welcome to ContracSel

A web based multi-criteria group decision support system for contractor selection using Fuzzy Analytic Hierarchy Process (FAHP)

The nature of contractor selection decision making problem is generally complicated and unstructured and there are many quantitative and qualitative criteria that must be considered to identify the appropriate contractor. This research aims to prioritize contractor selection criteria and rank the contractors taking into consideration all of the essential criteria by utilizing Fuzzy Analytic Hierarchy Process (FAHP). The system will aid timely decision making and reduce bias on the part of the stakeholders while allowing participation in an online platform.

ContracSel Web Portal

A Fuzzy enabled web based multi-criteria group decision support system for contractor selection

Selection of the best Contractor in the public procurement process is a typical example of a Multi-Criteria Decision Making Problem. In this research work, the Federal Universities in Nigeria will be used as case study. The proposed system will help in selecting highly qualified Contractors within a limited time for procurement in Nigeria Universities.
